# Company Policy Handbook

## Remote Work Policy
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week. Remote work must be pre-approved by the team lead for any duration exceeding 5 consecutive days. All remote employees must be reachable during core hours of 10:00 AM – 4:00 PM in their local timezone and must attend all scheduled team meetings via video call.

## Leave Policy
Full-time employees are entitled to 20 days of paid annual leave per year, which resets on January 1st. Unused leave of up to 10 days can be carried over to the next year. Sick leave is separate and capped at 10 days per calendar year. Leave requests must be submitted at least 5 business days in advance through the HR portal.

## Code of Conduct
All employees are expected to treat colleagues, clients, and partners with respect and professionalism. Harassment, discrimination, and bullying of any kind are strictly prohibited and may result in termination. Employees must report any violations to HR or their manager immediately. Confidential reporting channels are available on the intranet.

## Expense Reimbursement
Work-related expenses under ₹5,000 can be reimbursed directly through the finance portal with receipts. Amounts above ₹5,000 require prior approval from a department head. All reimbursement claims must be submitted within 30 days of the expense date. Travel expenses for client visits are fully reimbursable with manager approval.

## Equipment Policy
Each employee is provided a company laptop on Day 1. Accessories such as monitors, keyboards, and mice may be requested through IT. Employees are responsible for the safe handling of company equipment. Lost or damaged equipment due to negligence may be partially charged to the employee. Returning equipment upon resignation is mandatory.
